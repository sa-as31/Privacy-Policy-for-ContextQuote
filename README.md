Privacy Policy for ContextQuote
Last Updated: February 23, 2026

1. Introduction (简介)
ContextQuote is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles data.
ContextQuote 致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了我们的 Chrome 扩展程序如何处理数据。

2. Data Collection (数据收集)
Personal Data: ContextQuote does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII), such as names, email addresses, or browsing history.

个人数据： ContextQuote 不收集任何个人身份信息（PII），如姓名、电子邮件地址或浏览记录。

Selection Data: The text you select to quote is processed locally on your device to generate the reference. We do not transmit this content to any external servers.

划选数据： 您选取的引用文字仅在您的本地设备上处理以生成引用。我们不会将此内容传输到任何外部服务器。

3. Permissions (权限使用说明)
To provide its core functionality, ContextQuote requires the following permissions:
为了提供核心功能，ContextQuote 需要以下权限：

activeTab / tabs: Used only to retrieve the URL and title of the current page for citation purposes.

用于获取当前页面的 URL 和标题以进行引用标注。

storage: Used only to save your local settings and preferences (if any).

仅用于保存您的本地设置和偏好。

4. Third-Party Services (第三方服务)
ContextQuote does not share data with third-party services.
ContextQuote 不与任何第三方服务共享数据。

5. Changes to This Policy (政策更新)
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes.
我们可能会不时更新隐私政策。建议您定期查看此页面。

6. Contact Us (联系我们)
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系我们。
